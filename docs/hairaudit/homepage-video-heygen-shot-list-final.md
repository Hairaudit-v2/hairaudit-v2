# HairAudit homepage video — HeyGen-ready shot list (final)

**Copy revision (v2 refinements):** Scene 1 opens on *confidence for life*; Scene 2 uses *place their trust*; Scene 4 uses *That is exactly why HairAudit was created*; Scene 6 leads with *Developed by specialists… global training experience*; Scene 8 CTA VO uses **Verify your procedure today** (Option A), with B/C documented below.

**Target runtime:** 90 seconds  
**Aspect ratio:** 16:9 (primary web / paid social); 9:16 optional cut-down not covered here  
**Voice direction:** Calm, intelligent, authoritative — investigative documentary (not sales)  
**Visual reference:** Premium launch pacing (Apple / Tesla) — black, white, surgical macro, restrained motion  

**HeyGen usage (recommended):**

- **Scenes 1–3, 5, 8:** VO + editorial B-roll (mirror, surgery, donor macro, UI screen capture). HeyGen optional for a **brief** on-camera line in Scene 1 only if you want a face early; otherwise keep VO-only for gravity.
- **Scenes 4, 6, 7:** Strong for **presenter / expert avatar** (medium close-up, dark neutral background) intercut with product UI — or full VO with typography-only if you prefer zero face until Scene 6.

## Handoff checklist for HeyGen + editor

1. Export **VO script** as a single plain-text block for TTS or human record; keep ellipses where written for natural micro-pauses. Final CTA line in VO: **Verify your procedure today** (see Scene 8 for Options B/C if you A/B test).
2. For **HeyGen Video**: one project per “presenter segment” (Scenes 4, 6, 7) with **dark neutral** background, **medium close-up**, **slow** gestures; stitch in Resolve / Premiere with B-roll scenes.
3. **Music:** enter at 0:02–0:03; dip under VO in Scenes 4 and 8; no wall-of-sound under lists in Scene 5.
4. **Legal / claims:** “Independent verification” and medical imagery must match actual product and regulatory posture in each market.
