HairAudit homepage film - one topic: independent hair transplant verification for patients. Distribution: 16:9 web hero.

TARGET DURATION: Approximately 90 seconds. Premium launch pacing (restrained, serious) - not salesy, not upbeat SaaS.

NARRATION and PRESENTER: Calm, intelligent, authoritative English (neutral or British). Investigative documentary tone. Intercut a professional presenter (medium close-up, dark neutral studio) with stock/medical B-roll and motion graphics as scripted below. The presenter should feel like medical authority, not a salesperson.

This script is a concept and theme to convey - not a verbatim transcript. You have full creative freedom to tighten or bridge lines for flow, but do not change the meaning of clinical claims. Do not pad with dead air. Aim for ~90 seconds total.

MEDIA: Stock footage for mirror anxiety, surgical prep macro (non-gore), donor zone implication, consultation; motion graphics for typography, dashboards, report UI, subtle AI scan lines; avoid cartoon explainers, neon colors, cheap icons. No bright generic healthcare stock.

---

SCENE 1 (~10s) - Hook. B-roll emotional + surgical macro. VO:

A hair transplant can restore confidence for life. But when performed badly... it can permanently damage your future options forever. And most patients have no way of knowing whether their procedure was actually done correctly.

SCENE 2 (~12s) - Risk montage (faster cuts). VO:

Every year, thousands of patients place their trust in clinics with one of the most limited resources they have. Their donor area. Poor extraction... damaged grafts... poor surgical handling... and aggressive overharvesting... can compromise results permanently.

SCENE 3 (~12s) - Tension. VO:

The reality is simple. Most patients only discover something went wrong months later. When growth fails. When density is poor. When the damage can no longer be reversed.

SCENE 4 (~12s) - Product reveal. Presenter + sleek product visuals. VO:

That is exactly why HairAudit was created. Protecting patients through independent hair transplant verification. A forensic review platform designed to objectively analyse the quality and integrity of hair transplant procedures.

SCENE 5 (~12s) - Analysis. UI / graft / report texture. VO:

Using advanced AI analysis and expert surgical review... HairAudit evaluates: graft quality, donor management, implantation consistency, surgical technique, and procedural risk factors that directly impact long-term results.

SCENE 6 (~12s) - Authority. Presenter or clinical stills. VO:

Developed by hair restoration specialists with decades of surgical, clinical and global training experience... HairAudit was created to bring something this industry desperately needs. Transparency. Accountability. Confidence.

SCENE 7 (~12s) - Who it helps. VO:

Whether you are planning surgery... have already had a procedure... or simply want confidence before trusting a clinic... HairAudit helps protect your most valuable asset. Your donor.

SCENE 8 (~8s) - Black field, logo, URL. VO:

Before you trust a hair transplant clinic... know the truth. Verify your procedure today. Because your donor only gives you one chance.
